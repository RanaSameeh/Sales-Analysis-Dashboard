# Sales-Analysis-Dashboard
Sales Analysis Dashboard (Excel)
Project Overview
This project presents an interactive Sales Analysis Dashboard built in Excel, designed to extract meaningful insights from transactional data and support data-driven decision-making.

Objectives
Analyze overall sales performance and profitability
Identify high-performing and underperforming products
Evaluate regional (state-level) customer distribution
Understand the impact of discount strategies on profit
Build an interactive and user-friendly dashboard using Excel
Key Features

KPIs Overview
Total Sales: $1.1M+
Total Profit: $230K+
Total Quantity: 2,363
Average Discount: 13%
 
 Category Analysis
Sales, Profit, and Quantity breakdown by category
Identification of margin imbalances
 Product Insights
Top 10 and Bottom 10 products by sales
Sub-category profit analysis
 
 Geographical Analysis
Customer distribution across U.S. states
Identification of high- and low-performing regions
 Interactive Filters
Category selection
Year-wise filtering (2015–2018)
Quarterly analysi

Key Insights
Technology is the most profitable category, despite lower sales volume compared to Office Supplies
Furniture generates sales but contributes minimal profit → indicates margin issues
Office Supplies dominate in quantity but have lower profitability
A small number of states (e.g., California, Texas, New York) drive the majority of customers
Discounting (13% average) has a noticeable impact on profit margins
